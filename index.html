RNA Engineering Lab

because RNA matters



We Believe RNA Is Key To Understanding Biology

Our current inquiries revolve around three main themes: structure, regulation and artificial design.
We are part of the Institute for Research in Immunology and Cancer and the Computer Science Department of Université de Montréal.

Using mirBook, new methods of microRNA multi-gene targeting are currently under development.
